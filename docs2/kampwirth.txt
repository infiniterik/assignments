# Answer

# Research Paper Proposal: LGBT Politics in Latin America

## Research Questions:
1. How has the political landscape in Latin America influenced the advancement of LGBT rights?
2. What strategies have been employed by the LGBT community in Latin America to gain political power and influence?
3. To what extent have these strategies been successful in achieving tangible improvements in the lives of LGBT individuals?

## Bibliography:
1. Epstein, Steven. 1999. “Gay and Lesbian Movements in the United States: Dilemmas of Identity, Diversity and Political Strategy.” In Adam, Barry et al. The Global Emergence of Gay and Lesbian Politics. Philadelphia: Temple University Press. pp. 30-90.
2. Corrales, Javier. 2010. "The Politics of LGBT Rights in Latin America: Progress and Backlash in Argentina and Chile." Comparative Politics 42(4): 411-429.
3. Altman, Dennis. 2001. "Global Gaze/Global Gays." GLQ: A Journal of Lesbian and Gay Studies 7(4): 477-486.
4. Cruz-Malavé, Arnaldo. 2015. "Queer Latinidad: Identity Practices, Discursive Spaces." GLQ: A Journal of Lesbian and Gay Studies 21(2-3): 305-325.
5. Carrillo, Hiram. 2002. "The Night Is Young: Sexuality in Mexico in the Time of AIDS." GLQ: A Journal of Lesbian and Gay Studies 8(3): 377-400.

Note: The above bibliography consists of books and refereed journal articles that provide insights into the political dynamics of LGBT communities in Latin America.
